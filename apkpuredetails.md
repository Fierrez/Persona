## Short Description
All-in-one encrypted vault, 2FA authenticator, and personal task planner.

## Full Description
Persona is your ultimate personal security and productivity companion. Designed with privacy as a core principle, Persona combines a high-security vault with a powerful task planner and a seamless 2FA authenticator. All your data is encrypted locally on your device using military-grade AES encryption, ensuring that you—and only you—have access to your information.

### Key Features:

**1. Secure Vault & Password Manager**
- Store your login credentials, credit card details, and recovery codes.
- Organize entries with categories like Social, Work, Finance, and Games.
- Built-in Password Generator to create strong, unique passwords.
- Secure Notes with Markdown support for sensitive information.

**2. 2FA Authenticator**
- Protect your accounts with Time-based One-Time Passwords (TOTP).
- High-speed QR Code scanner for instant account setup.
- Real-time progress indicators for code rotations.
- Tap-to-copy functionality for a seamless login experience.

**3. Task Planner & Schedule**
- Stay organized with a dedicated task manager.
- Calendar view to track your daily and weekly schedule.
- Set priorities (High, Medium, Low) and due times for every task.
- Filter tasks by status: Recent, Pending, or Completed.

**4. Privacy & Security First**
- Local Encryption: Data never leaves your device unencrypted.
- Biometric Lock: Support for fingerprint and face authentication.
- Screen Protection: Prevents screenshots and keeps your data hidden from recent apps.
- No Tracking: We don't collect any personal data or usage analytics.

**5. Backup & Portability**
- Encrypted Backups: Create password-protected backups of your entire dataset.
- JSON Export: Portable data format for advanced users.
- Easy Import: Import credentials from CSV files or previous Persona backups.

### Permissions:
- **Camera:** Required for scanning 2FA QR codes.
- **Storage/Media:** Required for saving and importing backup files.
- **Biometrics:** Optional, for securing app access.

Take control of your digital persona today. Secure, private, and productive.
